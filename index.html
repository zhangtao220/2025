Combinatorics, Information Theory & Related Topics Seminar @ Xidian University

The seminar mainly hosts speakers in the fields of combinatorics, information theory and related topics. Topics of interest include, but are not limited to, combinatorial designs, algebraic combinatorics, graph theory, extremal combinatorics, finite geometry, discrete geometry, coding theory and cryptography.

Organizer: Tao Zhang

Time: Thursday, 9:30am-11:30am UTC
